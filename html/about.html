I am currently studying B.tech in Computer Science and engineering in VIT University, Vellore. I studied from M.P Birla H.S School. My hometown is Kolkata. I am also a web developer and programmer. I code in Java and python. I like playing cricket, and watching movies.

About VIT

VIT University is one of the finest engineering colleges in India. It has beautiful infrastructure. It has students hailing from all over India, thus providing a culturally rich environment. I met very talented students here. Most of the professors are very helpful. This college has given me many memories.

About M.P Birla School

M.P Birla H.S School, situated in Kolkata, is one of the best secondary schools in the city. It aims at academic as well as personality excellence. I made many life time friends here.

About Kolkata

Kolkata is my hometown. It's a big and congested city. Both rich and poor exists simultaneously here. Its rightly called the "city of joy" because all the people, irrespective of their caste or religion forget all their problems and unite to celebrate various festivals. It has the historic victoria memorial. India's first underground metro was also buit in this city.
